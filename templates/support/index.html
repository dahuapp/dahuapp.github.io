Support

Dahu is still in early stage of development. If you need some assistance with using Dahu, please feel free to ask a question by contacting the authors or by using our issues page.

Found a bug?

If you think you have found a bug, please raise it at https://github.com/dahuapp/Dahu/issues and we will take a look.

Requesting a new feature?

We're always interested in receiving suggestions on how to make Dahu even better. If you've got a feature request, please let us know at https://github.com/dahuapp/Dahu/issues and we will take a look.
